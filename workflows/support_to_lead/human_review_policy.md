# Human Review Policy

A case should be sent to human review when:

- The lead score is 70 or higher.
- The confidence score is below 0.75.
- The customer is from an enterprise or strategic account.
- The customer mentions legal, compliance, security, or procurement concerns.
- The message contains unclear or conflicting intent.
- The customer is upset but may still be a sales opportunity.

The AI may prepare a recommendation, but a human should approve the final sales handoff.
