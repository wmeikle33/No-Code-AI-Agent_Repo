# Failure Modes

## Employee-specific eligibility is unclear
Fallback: provide the general policy and recommend contacting HR.

## Policy is outdated
Fallback: flag the policy version and avoid definitive advice.

## Sensitive employee information requested
Fallback: refuse to expose private HR data and route to HR.

## Department-specific exception exists
Fallback: mention that local manager or department approval may override the general rule.
