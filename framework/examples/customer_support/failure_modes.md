# Failure Modes

## Customer message is vague
Fallback: ask for browser, account ID, screenshot, and steps to reproduce.

## Knowledge base has no matching article
Fallback: create a support ticket and route to technical triage.

## Service status is degraded
Fallback: acknowledge the incident and link the customer to the status page.

## Customer is angry or high-value
Fallback: increase priority and notify a human support manager.
