# Case Study: Lead Qualification Evaluation

## Business Problem

Some customers buy our product in bulk, this is is to evaluate whether or not they can be a good business partner.

## User Request

"I am interested in purchasing some of your product."

## Selected Agent

Lead Qualification Agent

## Why Coordinator Selected This Agent

The request was classified as:

- Customer inquiry
- Refund related
- Requires policy lookup

The routing confidence score was 0.96.

## Retrieved Knowledge

- refund_policy.md
- subscription_faq.md

"Refunds are allowed within 7 days of purchase."

Purchase amount: $29

Approval required: No

## Final Output

## Outcome

Ticket resolved automatically.
