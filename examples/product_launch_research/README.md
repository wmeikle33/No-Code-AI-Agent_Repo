# Case Study: Product Launch Research

## Business Problem

Prepare a launch recommendation for a new AI product.

## User Request

"Should we launch our product in healthcare or finance first?"

## Coordinator Decision

The coordinator decomposed the task into:

1. Market Research Agent
2. Literature Review Agent
3. KPI Monitoring Agent
4. Executive Assistant Agent

## Why These Agents Were Selected

Market Research Agent:
- Market size analysis

Literature Review Agent:
- Regulatory research

KPI Monitoring Agent:
- Historical performance metrics

Executive Assistant Agent:
- Final recommendation formatting

## Retrieved Knowledge

Internal:
- sales_data.csv
- previous_launches.md

External:
- healthcare market reports
- finance market reports

## Human Approval Point

Final recommendation reviewed by VP of Product.

## Final Output

Healthcare selected as initial launch market.

## Outcome

Recommendation package delivered to leadership.
